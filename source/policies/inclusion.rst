Community Statement on Inclusion
================================

At Bristol Hackspace, we’re proud to be a community built by our community for our community, a place where creativity, curiosity, and collaboration come together.

In our space everyone should feel welcome, respected, and safe to explore, make, and share ideas. Our community thrives when people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences are supported, encouraged and valued.

We celebrate diversity and are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where everyone can contribute and participate fully. Every member plays a key part in creating a space that reflects kindness, respect, and openness.

Bristol Hackspace has a zero tolerance approach to hate speech, bullying, or any form of behavior that undermines the wellbeing or dignity of others. We ask all members to uphold these values and help ensure that our space remains one where everyone feels they belong.

Should anyone feel that a member falls short of these values, you can approach either a committee member or a director and they will take action.

Together, we make Bristol Hackspace a safe space for all our community.
